Choripan

ingredients

  • 1-2 regular size chorizos
  • baguette style bread

step by step

  1. Make a few small incisions or pierce the chorizos and place them on a hot grill until they are fully cooked and golden. You can also cut the chorizos in half lengthwise or leave them whole. Sometimes they are cut in half after grilling and grilled again for a few minutes before serving.
  2. Corte el pan por la mitad y póngalos en la parrilla, con la parte interior hacia abajo, para que se calienten y se doren ligeramente.
  3. Coloque una cucharada o más de salsa chimichurri en el pan y agregue el chorizo. Cubra con salsa chimichurri adicional, curtido de cebolla y guacamole al gusto.
  4. To serve as picaditas or small snacks, you can cut the chorizo ​​and the bread into smaller pieces.
