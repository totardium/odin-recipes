Empanadas

ingredients

Step by step

  1. In a pot with hot oil add the onion and the bell pepper. Let brown for a few minutes and when they are half cooked (transparent onion), add the garlic and a little salt and pepper.
  2. Raise the heat and add all the minced meat at once. Move the meat so it doesn't stick. When the meat is sealed, add the diced tomato and the 2 tablespoons of tomato puree. Season with paprika and cumin, a little more salt and pepper and mix well. Cover (not completely, leave a small hole) and cook for half an hour, stirring little by little.
  3. Remove from the heat and let cool in the pot.In this step you can add anything else you want: hard-boiled egg, raisins, potatoes, etc.
  4. Distribute the filling in the empanada covers, and close with a traditional repulgue. Before putting them in the oven you can paint them with egg, but it is very comfortable and optional.
  5. Put our meat empanadas on a plate and bake in a strong oven until golden brown and enjoy!
